Chinese restaurant pictures Jia Jia Tangbao Location: 90 Huanghe Road (10 minute walk from the People Square) Price: RMB 28-35 per steamer Jia Jia Tangbao is a highly rated steamed soup dumpling restaurant in Shanghai. It features soup dumplings filling with shramp, pork, crab and so on. Crab dumplings are the most famous and expensive one in the restaurant because they re-package the meat from 250 kilos of crabs every day by hand. Yi Pin Ju Location: 423 Wulian road Price: RMB 200 per person Yi Pin Ju is a hotpot restaurant features lamb hotpot. Unlike a lot of other hotpot chains, which provide frozen sliced meat, Yi Pin Ju uses fresh lamb from Huzhou every night. Eating in this restaurant will make you warm and relaxed.
